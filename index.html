Uncompromising Quality, Guaranteed

We use only reputable paints and correct primers for every surface and finish. Our team adheres to manufacturer guidelines for spread rates and recoat times, maintaining wet edges to avoid lap marks, ensuring a standard two-coat system for a durable, beautiful finish. Low- or zero-VOC options are available upon request.

We stand by our work with a 1-year workmanship warranty covering peeling, blistering, or adhesion failure caused by our application. (Excludes moisture, structural movement, or pre-existing substrate issues.)
